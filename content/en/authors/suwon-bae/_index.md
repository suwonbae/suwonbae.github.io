Suwon Bae is an Assistant Professor in the [School of Mechanical Engineering](https://me.pusan.ac.kr) at Pusan National University (PNU).

Prior to joining PNU, he was a postdoctoral research associate (PI: [Dr. Kevin Yager](http://yager-research.ca)) in the [Center for Functional Nanomaterials](https://www.bnl.gov/cfn/) at Brookhaven National Laboratory. He received his Ph.D. from Cornell University under the supervision of [Prof. Meredith Silberstein](https://silbersteinlab.com) and his B.S. from Hanyang University.
