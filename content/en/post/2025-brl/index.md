BAE Research Group has been awarded a <strong>2025 Global Basic Research Laboratory (BRL) grant by the National Research Foundation of Korea (PI: Prof. Songkil Kim)</strong>.

On project titled "2D Material Dynamic Friction Interface Tribology Research Laboratory"</strong>, together with the principal investigator <strong>Prof. Songkil Kim</strong> (School of Mechanical Engineering) and the groups of <strong>Prof. Chibum Bahn and Prof. Junho Suh</strong>, we will conduct collaborative research from June 1, 2025 to May 31, 2028. 

Grant Information:\
Program: Global Research Laboratory (Advanced)\
Grant No.: RS-2025-02216195\
Title: 2D Material Dynamic Friction Interface Tribology Research Laboratory\
Period: 2025-06-01 – 2028-05-31
