Prof. Suwon Bae has been awarded an <strong>Early Career Research Grant (Type B) from the National Research Foundation of Korea</strong>.


From March 2026 to February 2031, the project titled <strong>"Development of a Particle-Based Molecular Dynamics Framework with Explicit Substrates and an Autonomous Experimentation Platform for Optimization of Directed Self-Assembly (DSA) Processes"</strong> will <strong>develop a particle-based molecular dynamics framework for ultra-fine semiconductor patterning process optimization and elucidate DSA defect control mechanisms</strong>.

Grant Information:\
Program: Excellence Research – Early Career Research (Type B)\
Grant No.: RS-2026-25493211\
Title: Development of an Explicit-Substrate Particle-Based Molecular Dynamics Simulation Framework and Autonomous Experimentation Platform for Directed Self-Assembly (DSA) Process Optimization\
Period: 2026-03-01 – 2031-02-28
